# Marketing Email

Owns marketing-email and single-send email surfaces.

## Power

- Trigger single-send marketing email operations.
- Coordinate marketing-email assets with campaigns and subscription preferences.

## Common Calls

- `POST /marketing/v3/transactional/single-email/send`
- Read asset metadata through marketing-email and related content APIs.

## Notes

- This is not the same as one-to-one sales email retrieval.
