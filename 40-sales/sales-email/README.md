# Sales Email

Owns read access to one-to-one email engagements.

## Power

- Retrieve content and metadata for one-to-one emails sent to contacts.
- Use this for auditing, reporting, or CRM context, not marketing-email sending.

## Common Calls

- `GET /crm/v3/objects/emails`
- `GET /crm/v3/objects/emails/{emailId}`
- `POST /crm/v3/objects/emails/search`
